# Nuroo Labs

> Research and experimentation in AI, machine learning, and assistive technologies for child development.

Nuroo Labs is the research and innovation arm of Nuroo, dedicated to exploring how artificial intelligence can support families, specialists, and organizations involved in child development.

Our mission is to build responsible AI systems that augment human expertise and improve access to developmental support.

---

## Mission

We envision a future where AI empowers:

- Families
- Specialists
- Educational organizations
- Child development centers

Technology should strengthen human relationships, not replace them.

---

## Research Areas

### Artificial Intelligence

- Personalized recommendations

### Machine Learning

- Predictive analytics
- Learning trajectories

### Computer Vision

- Video analysis

- Multimodal learning

## Responsible AI

Nuroo Labs follows the principles of:

- Human-centered AI
- Privacy by design
- Specialist-in-the-loop systems
- Transparency and safety
- Ethical use of AI

AI systems developed in Nuroo Labs are designed to assist professionals and families, not replace them.
